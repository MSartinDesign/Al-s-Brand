# Alís Layout Compliance Governance Rule

**Effective Date:** June 27, 2026  
**Applicability:** All digital interfaces, print collateral, editorial layouts, and marketing materials.  
**Objective:** To systematically evaluate and verify if a layout adheres to the "Quiet Luxury" and "Chef’s Atelier" visual standards set forth by the Brand Color, Typography, and Visual Guidelines.

---

## 1. Compliance Evaluation Matrix

A layout is deemed **Compliant** only if it satisfies 100% of the mandatory criteria across the three core brand pillars detailed below.

### Pillar A: Brand Colors & Structural Weights
* **The 80% White Canvas Rule:** The layout background must default to a solid, flat `#FFFFFF`. Artificial drop shadows, gradients, or off-white/tinted fills are strictly prohibited.
* **The 20% Black Structural Rule:** All structural accents—including typography, border frames, table lines, and user interface (UI) containers—must be rendered in solid `#000000`.
* **Line Weight Restraint:** Geometric divider lines and editorial rules must maintain a thin, deliberate thickness between **0.5pt and 1pt**.
* **Color Purity:** The layout must be completely free of unauthorized secondary accents, grays, or metallic faux-textures. Visual color must originate solely from embedded imagery or materials.

### Pillar B: Brand Typography & Hierarchy
* **Primary Font (Playfair Display Medium):** Reserved exclusively for Headlines, Editorial Callouts, Hero Statements, and Section Markers.
    * *Mandatory Styling:* Must be **ALLY CAPITALIZED** with expanded letter-spacing (`+5%` to `+10%` / `0.06em` to `0.1em`).
    * *Prohibition:* Never use for body copy, running text, or small labels.
* **Secondary Font (Inter):** Reserved for Subheadings (Semi-Bold), Body Narrative (Regular), Technical Data, and UI Components.
    * *Mandatory Styling:* Must use Sentence or Title case with a generous line spacing of **1.5× to 1.6×**.
    * *Prohibition:* Never use full capitalization for running body text.
* **High-Contrast Pairing:** The two fonts must never be mixed within a single running sentence. Maintain a strict linear separation (serifs for structural titles; sans-serifs for descriptions/parameters).

### Pillar C: Brand Visuals & Photographic Integration
* **The Passe-Partout Principle:** Photography must be framed with generous white margins (`#FFFFFF`) to mimic a premium, museum-grade lifestyle documentary asset.
* **Text-Free Imagery:** Standalone photographic assets must contain **zero text, brand logos, or graphic overlays** inside the photo frame.
* **Atmospheric Cohesion:** Embedded imagery must feature muted, earthy tones and soft, warm golden hour lighting (**2,000K to 3,500K**). Cold, blue, clinical white, or hyper-exposed tech-showroom aesthetics are strictly forbidden.

---

## 2. Gatekeeper Approval Checklist
Designers and brand managers must review the layout against this checklist prior to publication.

- [ ] **Is the background pure `#FFFFFF` without any gradients or shadows?**
- [ ] **Are all text and structural lines pure `#000000`?**
- [ ] **Are all Playfair Display headers fully capitalized and properly letter-spaced?**
- [ ] **Does the body text use Inter with a line height of at least 1.5×?**
- [ ] **Are the images free of graphic overlays and framed with generous whitespace?**
- [ ] **Does the layout successfully isolate and elevate the natural colors of food and materials?**

---

## 3. Governance Enforcement & Exceptions
> **Non-Compliance Protocol:** Any layout that incorporates gray tones, mixed typography inside a single line, unauthorized capitalization, or cluttered photographic backdrops fails compliance automatically and must be returned to the design stage. No exceptions are granted for seasonal campaigns; the monochromatic minimalism of Alís is absolute.
